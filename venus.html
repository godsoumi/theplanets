VENUS

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is a terrestrial planet and the only one of the Solar System with a substantial and thicker atmosphere other than Earth. Venus is the only Solar System object whith a size and mass close to Earth's. The surface is a rusty color and it's peppered with intensely crunched mountains and thousands of large volcanoes.
